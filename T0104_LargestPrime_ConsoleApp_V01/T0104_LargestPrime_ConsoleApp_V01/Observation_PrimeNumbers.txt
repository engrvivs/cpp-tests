Except for 2 and 3 and their respective multiples, rest of the prime numbers can be found by starting from 5 and incrementing by 6. Either the iterator or (iterator + 2) are probable candidates for being a prime number. This observation is found to be correct atleast till 100.

Prime Numbers types:
	1. Twin 
		- that differ by 2
	2. Cousin 
		- that differ by 4
	3. Sexy
		- that differ by 6
